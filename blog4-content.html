What Makes a Great Solo Leveling Alternative?

Before the list, here is exactly what made Solo Leveling special — and what to look for in alternatives:

  • The underdog rise — MC starts at rock bottom, earns every power-up
  • A unique system — leveling interface, stats, skills that only the MC has access to
  • Solo gameplay — the MC often operates alone or stands far above everyone else
  • World-building — gates, dungeons, monsters, hunter societies
  • Art quality — full-color panels that make fights feel like animated sequences

Every manhwa on this list hits at least four of these five marks. Some hit all five.

The 15 Best Manhwa Like Solo Leveling

1. The Beginning After the End
Genre: Fantasy / Reincarnation / Action
Completed OP MC Reincarnation

King Grey was the most powerful person alive — cold, lonely, and undefeated. Then he died and was reborn as Arthur Leywin in a world of magic. With all his battle knowledge and strategic genius, Arthur grows from a gifted child into a terrifying force. The emotional depth here is unmatched — Arthur has real relationships, real losses, and real growth. If Solo Leveling hooked you on power progression, The Beginning After the End will break your heart and rebuild it. Widely considered the closest thing to a Solo Leveling successor.

2. Second Life Ranker
Genre: Action / Fantasy / Tower
Ongoing Tower Climbing Revenge

Yeonwoo discovers his twin brother was betrayed and died inside a mysterious tower. Armed with his brother's secret journal, he enters the same tower to find the truth and destroy every person responsible. Second Life Ranker hits emotionally from chapter one — the protagonist is driven by grief and love, not just a desire for power. The system mechanics are creative, the fights are brutal, and the stakes feel personal throughout every arc. One of the most consistently excellent ongoing manhwa available.

3. Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint
Genre: Fantasy / Apocalypse / System
Completed Unique Premise Emotional

Kim Dokja is the only person who ever read a niche web novel to completion. Then the world transforms into that exact novel — monsters, scenarios, and death at every turn. Dokja alone knows how the story ends. This is the most emotionally complex manhwa on this list — smarter, deeper, and more devastating than Solo Leveling in many ways. The art style is gorgeous and the story rewards attentive readers with layers that keep revealing themselves. Many readers consider this the greatest manhwa ever written.

4. Nano Machine
Genre: Martial Arts / Action / System
Ongoing 300+ Chapters Martial Arts

A descendant of the heavenly demon cult receives a nano machine from the future — a technological system that gives him abilities no martial artist has ever had. In a world of brutal sects, deadly cultivation, and constant betrayal, he uses this edge to rise from the weakest student to the most feared warrior alive. Nano Machine is beloved for one reason above all — it never loses momentum. Hundreds of chapters in and the quality remains consistently excellent. Perfect for Solo Leveling fans who want the same satisfaction with a martial arts twist.

5. Tomb Raider King
Genre: Action / Comedy / System
Ongoing Relic System Time Regression

Mysterious tombs appear worldwide, each containing powerful relics that grant superhuman abilities. Seo Joo-Heon — the best relic hunter alive — gets betrayed and killed by his employer. Then he wakes up years in the past with complete knowledge of every tomb in existence. Tomb Raider King has one of the most entertaining protagonists in all of manhwa. The humor is sharp, the action is creative, and every chapter delivers. If you want something with the same power fantasy as Solo Leveling but with more personality and laughs, this is your series.

6. Doom Breaker
Genre: Action / Regression / Fantasy
Ongoing Regression Dark Themes

Zephyr fought until the very end of the world — and lost. After dying, he is sent back to the beginning with all his memories and power. Unlike many regression manhwa, Doom Breaker is brutally dark. Zephyr is not a gentle hero — he is a weapon shaped by watching humanity fail. The art is stunning, the fights are merciless, and the pacing is relentless. This is currently one of the hottest ongoing manhwa in 2026, especially popular among readers who want Solo Leveling's intensity cranked up even further.

7. The Legendary Mechanic
Genre: Sci-Fi / System / Action
Completed Unique Class Sci-Fi

A game developer wakes up inside the game he helped create — as an NPC with no special starting class. Using his knowledge of the game's mechanics, systems, and future events, he builds a path no player was ever supposed to walk. The Legendary Mechanic stands out for its creative use of the system genre — instead of pure combat power, the MC uses intelligence, preparation, and unconventional thinking. For Solo Leveling fans who want more strategic depth, this is essential reading.

8. I'm the Max-Level Newbie
Genre: Action / Game / Comedy
Ongoing Gaming System Funny MC

Kang Jinhyeok became the only person to ever complete the world's hardest tower game. Then the game became real — with humanity's survival at stake. As the only person with complete knowledge of every floor, every trap, and every boss, he is humanity's only hope. What makes this series special is the MC's self-awareness — he knows exactly how strong he is and acts accordingly. It captures Solo Leveling's power fantasy with added humor and a protagonist who is genuinely fun to read. A perfect entry point for new manhwa readers.

9. Return of the Mount Hua Sect
Genre: Martial Arts / Comedy / Action
Ongoing Best Martial Arts Comedy

Chung Myung — the greatest swordsman of his generation, the one who defeated the Demonic Cult leader — dies and reincarnates 100 years later. His sect is a shadow of what it was. His disciples' descendants are weak and directionless. He will fix everything. Return of the Mount Hua Sect is one of the funniest and most heartwarming manhwa being published. Do not let the comedy fool you — the fight scenes are spectacular and the underdog journey is deeply satisfying. One of the most beloved ongoing series in 2026.

10. Eleceed
Genre: Action / Superhero / Comedy
Ongoing Speed Powers Heartwarming

Jiwoo has superhuman reflexes and a heart that cannot stop helping people. One day he finds a powerful awakened warrior trapped inside a fat, fluffy cat. Their mentor-student partnership becomes one of the best dynamics in all of manhwa. Eleceed is funny, emotionally grounded, and increasingly spectacular in its action sequences — Jiwoo eventually moves at supersonic speeds, producing some of the most visually impressive fight panels in the genre. An anime adaptation is confirmed for 2026.

11. Solo Leveling: Ragnarok
Genre: Action / System / Fantasy
Ongoing Official Sequel 2026 Hit

The official sequel to Solo Leveling follows Suho — Sung Jin-Woo's son — stepping into a world that his father reshaped. If you loved the original, this is required reading. It expands the lore while delivering the high-octane battles that made the original legendary. Currently one of the most-read manhwa globally in 2026.

12. The Player That Can't Level Up
Genre: Action / System / Fantasy
Completed Unique System Mystery

Kim GiGyu awakened as a player five years ago but has been stuck at level one ever since — unable to level up no matter what he does. His system is broken. Or so everyone thinks. The truth behind why he cannot level up becomes one of the most satisfying reveals in manhwa. This series is tightly plotted, surprisingly emotional, and delivers a unique take on the system genre that feels fresh even after Solo Leveling set such a high standard.

13. Reincarnation of the Suicidal Battle God
Genre: Action / Regression / Fantasy
Ongoing Dark Fantasy Intense

Zephyr — the last survivor of humanity — is sent back in time after the demons destroy everything. Unlike most regression protagonists, Zephyr carries the full psychological weight of watching everyone he ever knew die. The combat is among the best drawn in manhwa and the emotional core hits harder than most pure action series. One of the most underrated manhwa currently publishing.

14. A Returner's Magic Should Be Special
Genre: Fantasy / Strategy / Action
Completed Smart MC Academy Setting

Desir Arman survived the Shadow Labyrinth — the dungeon that destroyed humanity — and was sent back in time thirteen years. He enrolls in a magic academy, determined to build the team that will save the world this time. This series balances strategy and action better than almost any other manhwa. The protagonist wins with intelligence as much as power, and the academy setting adds character dynamics that pure dungeon manhwa often lack.

15. Dungeon Reset
Genre: Action / Comedy / Survival
Ongoing Unique Premise Creative

Jung Dawoon gets trapped in a dungeon — but due to a system glitch, he is immune to resets, meaning traps no longer affect him and the dungeon environment becomes his personal playground. Where Solo Leveling is serious and relentless, Dungeon Reset is inventive and surprisingly funny. The MC crafts, builds, and engineers his way through dungeons rather than purely fighting. A refreshing alternative for readers who want the dungeon setting without the constant intensity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What manhwa is most similar to Solo Leveling?

The Beginning After the End and Doom Breaker are the closest matches in terms of power progression, emotional investment, and art quality. Second Life Ranker is also a top recommendation for the revenge and tower-climbing elements.

Is there a Solo Leveling sequel?

Yes — Solo Leveling: Ragnarok is the official sequel, following Sung Jin-Woo's son Suho. It launched in 2024 and is currently one of the most-read manhwa globally in 2026.

What is the system manhwa genre?

System manhwa refers to stories where the protagonist gains access to a game-like interface — stat screens, skill trees, level-up notifications — that only they can see. Solo Leveling popularized this format globally. Nano Machine, I'm the Max-Level Newbie, and The Player That Can't Level Up all use this mechanic creatively.

What is regression manhwa?

Regression manhwa features a protagonist who dies or reaches the end of a story and is sent back in time — usually to the beginning — with all their memories and sometimes powers intact. Doom Breaker, Tomb Raider King, and A Returner's Magic Should Be Special all use this premise. It is one of the fastest-growing subgenres in 2026.

Which manhwa on this list has the best art?

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint, The Beginning After the End, and Doom Breaker are consistently praised for their art quality. Solo Leveling: Ragnarok also maintains the high visual standard of the original series.
